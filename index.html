About

My name is Suzanna, and I'm a first year information science major in Chapel Hill's School of Information and Library Science. In this program, I hope to advance my understanding of the way humans interact with information, and how to make information increasingly accessible in the age of technology. I also hope to gain valuable experience with the experts within the school and the research triangle area, continually building my skillset and resume.
